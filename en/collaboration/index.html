Cloud-based, Microsoft 365 is constantly evolving. In particular, application updates are managed entirely by Microsoft.


Migration, management and operation


Innobyte masters all the building blocks of Microsoft 365 and is therefore able to install all or part of the solution: Migration, Management and Operation hold no secrets for us! Around the Microsoft 365 solution, Innobyte has developed three offers to help you manage your Microsoft 365 environment: A complete migration solution, drawing on the expertise of numerous migrations. To support you in managing your Microsoft 365 environment, Innobyte offers two options for Microsoft 365 operation and security, and an OnDemand offering, totally tailored to your needs and issues.


Project management


Innobyte teams are with you every step of the way. A dedicated project manager monitors the schedule and provides you with reports and a technical architecture file. The teams then take charge of the creation of the Microsoft 365 tenant, and lead the pilot phase from which the roll-out plan is derived.


Migration


Migration is carried out gradually, using hybrid or migration tools. After validation of the IT pilot, Innobyte teams will assist your company with the VIP migration. Following the migration, a skills transfer is provided to the people in charge of Microsoft 365 management.


Documentation


Deliverables include: Project Vision / Scope, Requirements Documentation, Detailed Design, Deployment Plan, Test Book and operating documents. A provisional schedule is also systematically delivered.


Security & advanced security


Innobyte teams can implement classic security solutions or advanced security with EMS; in addition, customers benefit from the permanent presence of Innobyte's SOC.


User support


Support, located in Algeria, responds to users' technical and usage issues, guaranteeing a rapid response from 08:00 to 18:00. During this period, incident handling takes less than 30 minutes.


Accompanying change


A project manager pays you a monthly visit, and we put at your disposal via a platform: courses on specific themes, courses for a structured teaching approach, tutorials, usage videos...


Collaboration

Synchronization & Backup

Antivirus and protection


Centralized management and administration of your endpoints in SaaS mode! In the face of increasing cyberattacks and their growing sophistication, businesses need the enhanced capabilities of an endpoint protection platform to protect themselves. Laptops, desktops, web servers... all these devices are potential entry points for attacks. That's why it's vital to install a protection system on each and every one of them.


Choose intelligent endpoint protection


With Innobyte's AlterProtect offer, opt for intelligent, optimized and interconnected endpoint protection to secure all your work environments.


Your sensitive data is protected, and malware and targeted attacks such as spam, phishing, viruses, spyware and unwanted content are detected and stopped before they can impact your business.
