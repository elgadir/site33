Innobyte hosts your systems


Innobyte’s Private Cloud offer gives you the opportunity to create a bespoke, perfectly adapted and dedicated information system. Guaranteeing a high level of security, these architectures are also fully interconnectable and authorize hybrid management to the Public Cloud (Carrier-neutral, operator services, “GéoTransparence”…). Implemented in Algeria on several fully owned sites, Innobyte’s Data Centers support resilient and upgradable solutions, offering an ideal environment for any digital ecosystem.


Infrastructure security


All Innobyte Data Centers have secure uninterrupted power supply, redundant air conditioning and 3M Novec fire suppression systems.


Access security is guaranteed with the use of magnetic and biometric controls, CCTV, on-site personnel and private storage bays.


System security


The hosted architectures can be deployed in BCP or DRP mode over several Data Centers connected by private fiber optics.


These level 2 interconnections authorize the construction of highly resilient solutions (stretched-cluster) allowing automatic load balancing and extremely low RTOs.


Data security


Innobyte’s Security Operation Center monitors, detects and acts on cybersecurity threats and incidents.


The SOC also performs customized audits and pentests and assesses regulatory compliance. All these solutions are bundled together in Falcon, Bastion, Sentinel and Javelin


Supervision


The Network Operation Center provides 24/7 monitoring of the systems and incident management.


The NOC is made up of teams which are all contracted to Innobyte and located on its premises.


Interconnection


Innobyte offers multi-operator solutions that provide a wide range of link services (Fiber, copper, 4G, VPN, N2, N3…) allowing its clients to interconnect with its Data Centers and with the Internet.


Hybrid management


With its multi-operator backbone and its private links, Innobyte can connect the Private Cloud and the Public Cloud in a high performing and secure manner.


Its “GéoTransparence” solution allows the information system to be extended regardless of the point of presence or the location of the data.

Innobyte's Power OnDemand offer


Because of the mission-critical applications they host, Power System platforms (IBM i and Unix) still occupy a strategic position within IT Departments, and must also be part of companies' unavoidable digital transformation plans. The power of the Power System, the flexibility of the Cloud


Because of the mission-critical applications they host, Power System platforms (IBM i and Unix) still occupy a strategic position within IT departments. At the same time, however, they must also be part of companies' unavoidable digital transformation plans.


Innobyte's Power OnDemand offering meets these challenges with a powerful, reliable and flexible IBM i / Unix Cloud solution.


Reduce your operational costs, improve security and performance... Our solution is based on more than 20 years of expertise, acquired in partnership with IBM and its ecosystem.


As a major player in hosting and managed services, Innobyte offers you a tailor-made solution to meet the requirements of Power systems and your digital transformation challenges.


Major assets



  • Reduce your operating costs

  • Benefit from state-of-the-art platforms

  • High availability and uninterrupted maintenance

  • Customize and adapt power only when needed

  • Enhance the security and integrity of your data


Performance


Power OnDemand is built on latest-generation platforms (IBM Power 9, SSD NVME disk arrays, etc.) providing the power and robustness required for your mission-critical applications.


As a telecom operator, Innobyte also provides you with secure, high-speed links, guaranteeing end-to-end access performance.


Availability


The fully redundant architecture between two datacenters makes it easy to set up a BCP or DRP, essential for maintaining your critical production environments. Innobyte's datacenters meet the Tier III standard of the Uptime Institute (or its equivalent in the EN 506000 standard), and feature hot-swap maintenance on all components.


Flexibility


Power OnDemand is a simple, cost-effective way to keep pace with business growth, or to meet specific power requirements. Performance and capacity planning reports enable you to visualize workloads and load periods, so you can adapt power only when it's needed.


Data security


Innobyte has all the certifications required to ensure the security of hosted data (ISO27001, SOC 1 Type 2, SOC 2 Type 2, HDS, PCI DSS...). What's more, our BCP/ERP solutions and backup systems based on capacitive robotics reinforce the security and integrity of your sensitive data.


Managed services


Innobyte's teams of power experts handle all or part of the day-to-day operating tasks. They also respond to incidents reported via the 24-hour, 365-day-a-year supervision service. IBM i or Unix project managers take charge of implementation or migration operations to Power OnDemand, using a proven methodology tailored to business constraints.


Location in Algeria


Innobyte offers you a 100% Made in Algeria solution. All our teams live and work in Algeria (Nanterre, Issy-les-Moulineaux, Montigny-le-Bretonneux).
