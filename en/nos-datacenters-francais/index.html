    Why choose a French datacenter?

    Safety, compliance and performance.

    Enhanced safety

    Security is a top priority at Innobyte's French data centers. Our centers stand out for their strict security standards and rigorous data protection regulations.

    Regulatory compliance

    French data centers are distinguished by their adherence to French and European standards, offering rigorous compliance with rules and directives established at national and European level. These centers emphasize compliance with current regulations, guaranteeing strict compliance with the data security and management standards imposed by national and European laws. This compliance is essential to ensure the protection and confidentiality of stored information.

    The protection of personal data is at the heart of the concerns of French data centers, putting forward strict data protection regulations. These regulations provide a solid legal framework to guarantee the confidentiality of hosted personal information. Data centers strive to scrupulously comply with these strict regulations to ensure maximum protection of personal data, thus meeting the compliance and security requirements imposed by national and European data protection authorities.

    Performance and benefits

    French data centers stand out for their state-of-the-art infrastructures, featuring innovative advances to guarantee high-performance, secure data management.

    These technological advances ensure optimum reliability in the storage, transmission and protection of information, providing a secure environment in which to safeguard crucial corporate data. These centers incorporate state-of-the-art technologies such as efficient cooling solutions, robust security systems and sophisticated network infrastructures, ensuring excellent data management and high-level protection.

    In terms of reliability and performance, French data centers are often ahead of international solutions. Their reputation for enhanced stability and high performance makes them the preferred choice for hosting data requiring optimum levels of security and accessibility. Thanks to effective safeguards, rigorous security protocols and state-of-the-art technologies, these data centers deliver consistent, reliable performance, meeting the most stringent corporate requirements for data storage and processing.

    This superior reliability and performance positions French data centers at the forefront of reliable and efficient options for enterprise data management, playing an essential role in the preservation, security and optimal management of strategic information. These advantages make French data centers major assets for companies looking for reliable solutions for safeguarding and processing their critical data.
